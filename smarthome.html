Process

From Idea to Smart Home

01 — Consultation

Needs analysis, budget planning, and recommendation of a suitable system architecture.

02 — Planning

Detailed planning with floor plan, device list, wiring diagram, and protocol selection.

03 — Installation

Setting up Home Assistant, integrating all devices, and configuring automations.

04 — Visualization

Creating a dashboard with floor plan, control elements, and system overviews.

05 — Handover

Training all residents, documentation, and handing over administration access.

06 — Support

Optional maintenance contract for updates, extensions, and troubleshooting.

Smart Home Features

Lighting

  • Dimmable LED spots (Zigbee) with color temperature control

  • Scenes: Movie Night, Reading, Guests, Away

Media

  • Multi-room audio (Sonos / Home Assistant Media Player)

Climate

  • Smart radiator valves (Zigbee)
  • Temperature and humidity sensor
  • Automatic heating control based on presence

Automation

  • Blinds close at sunset
  • Heating turns down when window is open
